## 1. Introduction & Objectives
### Objective:
Ensure D-Chipset App meets requirements for supporting Hearing Aids (RIC, BTE, ITE).

## 2. Scope
### In Scope:
- Functional requirements (FR)
- Non-functional requirements (NFR)
- User stories
- Firmware interaction (Bluetooth LE Audio, Classic BT)

### Out of Scope:
- Hardware manufacturing
- Firmware development (unless related to App integration)

## 3. Test Types & Levels
- **Unit Testing**
- **Integration Testing** (App + Firmware simulator/Real HA)
- **System Testing** (End-to-End with iOS/Android devices)
- **System Maturity Test (SMT)**

## 4. Test Environment Requirements
- **Mobile Devices**: Android 10+, iOS 15+
- **Hearing Aids**: RIC, BTE, ITE models
- **Accessories**: TV Streamer, Remote Control

## 5. Risk Analysis & Mitigation
- Bluetooth connectivity issues
- Audio streaming latency/distortion
- Battery drain
